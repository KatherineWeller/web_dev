Opinion: Call of Duty Updates Too Frequently

The update i'm currently watching is 21 GB... why do I need to download >20 GB worth of content every single time I open the game?

There isn't even a significant difference after the update.

Problems with this:

  1. I have to schedule playing to allow for ~1 hr worth of download time before I can even hop into a game.
  2. The Playstation 4 has 500 GB of memory, this game already accounts for ~100 GB of that memory.
      3. This takes away from space for other games.

Maybe this is just how games are going to operate now, as the players demand ever-updating content and developers work to keep audiences engaged.
